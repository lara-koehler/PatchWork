The parameter map

Every assembly comes from a recipe with two dials: how sticky particles are with one another on average, and how much their patches vary in stickiness from one another. Each dot below is one simulated recipe, colored by what it grew into and placed by its two dial settings. Hover a dot to preview it, click to explore it up close.

Simulation data from Phys. Rev. X 14, 041061 (2024). See the science page for more.
