A gallery of self-assembled particles

Every tile below is a simulated swarm of 500 particles that were free to stick together however their patches allowed. Some barely noticed each other; others locked into rigid crystals, tangled into gels, or spun out into long fibers. Click any tile to explore it up close.

Simulation data from Phys. Rev. X 14, 041061 (2024). See the science page for more.
